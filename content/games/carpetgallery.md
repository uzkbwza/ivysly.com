An endless walker with procedural scenery and objects. Please remove footwear upon entry. Made for Toy Jam 1 with [fibr/faec](https://slagsat.bandcamp.com/) doing the music.

- **arrow keys/WASD**: move
- **Z/J**: interact/confirm
- **X/K**: map/cancel
- **C/L**: screenshot 
- **Esc**: menu
- **F11**: toggle fullscreen
- the same world is shared by all players
- executable saves screenshots to My Pictures
- web version may have audio problems and other bugs. download for the best experience
