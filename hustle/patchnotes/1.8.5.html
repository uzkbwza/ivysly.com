Ninja

  • Store Momentum

    • Reduced IASA (15f -> 13f)

  • Uppercut (grounded)

Cowboy

Wizard

Robot

  • Currently Robot's playstyle is defined by choosing extremely safe options that have higher reward than the other characters'. The risk should more closely match the reward.
  • Fixed blockstun not being applied to Robot when blocking after autoguard.
  • Can no longer fly and parry melee attacks at the same time. Can still fly + block and parry projectiles.

  • Blocking a melee attack disables flight.
