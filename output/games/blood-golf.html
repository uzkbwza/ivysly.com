Blood Golf 2023

<< 27 Aug 2023 >>

BLOOD GOLF is a PVP or PVE golf-em-up where your target is the other player. Use powerups, map knowledge, and, most importantly, elegant combat-golf technique to turn your enemies into a fine mist or send them past the stratosphere. Has local multiplayer, or you can fight against the CPU. I made this game with blauxel who drew all the art.
